Different types of organizations

There are many different types of localization organizations, and they have different specialties, missions and target audience.

Professional

GALA Global, IMUG, Women in Localization, Localization Institute, etc.

Business-oriented

LocWorld, Nimdzi, etc.

Academic

Translators Association of China, Common Sense Advisory, etc.

Technology-driven

TAUS, International Association for Machine Translation (including AMTA, AAMT, EAMT), etc.

Humanitarian

Translators Without Borders, Translation Commons, etc.

Others

There are also other types of localization organizations.
